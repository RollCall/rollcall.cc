Roll Call is a San Francisco-based casual cycling club composed mostly of software designers and engineers. Formed in 2012, we typically value camaraderie over speed — but we're not opposed to mashing up Hawk Hill when the opportunity arises.

Our flagship ride, a two-hour affair over varied terrain, departs from the Panhandle (Fell & Baker) at 6:45 AM on Wednesdays. A longer ride is generally created ad-hoc for Sunday morning spinning.

The best way to get in touch is to come ride, but feel free to check us out on Strava, follow us on Twitter, or shoot us an email.
